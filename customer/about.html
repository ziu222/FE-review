Our Story

Founded in 2022 in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City, EC SHOP was born from a single conviction: Vietnamese consumers deserve access to world-class electronics without compromise — in quality, in price, or in experience.

What started as a small storefront has grown into a trusted platform serving tens of thousands of customers across Vietnam, guided by an unrelenting obsession with curation, authenticity, and service.
